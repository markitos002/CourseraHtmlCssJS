G8 Summit protests

Public demonstrations

Protesters gathered in the streets of London to demonstrate against the G8 Summit.

Police officers were deployed to maintain order and ensure the safety of all participants.

Violent clashes

Some protests turned violent, with clashes between demonstrators and law enforcement.

Several arrests were made as a result of the violence.
